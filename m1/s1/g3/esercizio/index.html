Focaccia with Caramelized Onion, Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holyday starter!

image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cup (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoon istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoon salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 teablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries
